About Me

I am currently enrolled in the University of Minnesota's coding bootcamp for full stack web development. After engaging in a variety of volunteer and paid work, as well as attending one year of college while being a stay-at-home parent, I decided to re-enter the workforce as a web developer.

I am creative, detail-oriented, and enjoy troubleshooting. I really enjoy the challenging, yet satisfying world of coding.

When not learning code, I like to go for walks by the river, photograph the fascinating and beautiful parts of the world, create art, and connect with friends.
